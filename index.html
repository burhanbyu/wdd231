About KCC

The Kenya Chamber of Commerce (KCC) is a trade institution that is working to protect commercial and industrial interests of Kenyan business community. KCC advocates for the creation of a favorable commercial, trade and investment environment that supports enterprise expansion. The membership of KCC constitutes small, micro enterprises (MSEs), medium and large enterprises.
